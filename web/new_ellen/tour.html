Enabling people to network effectively can be hard

Using a fun, algorithm-driven filtering system, CrowdScanner creates an attendee visualisation that becomes a social object at your event, real time, so people can figure out who they need to be talking to, and what to talk to them about.

Customise Questions

It couldn't be easier to get started with CrowdScanner. Create 5 multiple choice questions & upload your logo.

Within the CrowdScanner Dashboard, you can customise the visualisation of attendee information. The questions can be a mix between funny, functional and fictional, and CrowdScanner will help you on your creative journey.

Customise your Questions

Share Questions

Now that you've created your questions, it's time to get your attendees involved. It's quick and painless. Send them the permalink. That's it!

They have the choice to answer using their ipad, iphone or through the web. If you want to make sure your attendees participate, post it on your website for easy access.

Set up the Display

Now you can set up a projector and screens at your event so you can display the visualisations.

CrowdScanner works by using social objects: by placing visualisations of the attendee answers around your event, you can create hot spots where people can gather to talk and analyse the results. You will have people joining in on the day so as to not feel left out.

Let People Talk

The attendees have access to a personal graph on their iphone/ipad devices, and can use the inbuilt sms functionality to text a certain group of people to specify a meet up time.

CrowdScanner enables your attendees to take control of their own networking experience. Now you can sit back, relax, and let them do what they came to your event to do: learn from each other, exchange ideas, and meet the right people.
