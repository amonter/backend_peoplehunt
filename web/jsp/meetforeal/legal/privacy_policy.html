Meetforeal Privacy Policy

Information Collected by meetforeal.ie / meetforeal.com

When you register with Meetforeal you submit information such as your name, email address, age etc. To a certain point, you decide how much information you want to share about yourself.

Use of information

Information such as your username, name, age, gender etc will be displayed on Meetforeal as well as your personality profile and details (which is really more for fun). Most of the information about yourself is however really up to you choose whether you want to disclose or not. We will use your email address to contact you from time to time. We may use non-personally identifiable information, like your IP address, to statistically analyze site usage and to customize our site's content, layout and services. The resulting information will allow us to improve our site and better understand and serve our users. We will disclose your personally identifiable information if we reasonably believe we are required to do so by law, regulation or other government authority or to protect the rights and property of meetforeal, its affiliates or the public. We may also cooperate with law enforcement agencies in any official investigation and we may disclose your personally identifiable information to the relevant agency in doing so.

Third Party Advertising

Meetforeal may share profile information in a personally identifiable manner and aggregate usage information in a non-personally identifiable manner to advertisers and other third parties in order to present to members more targeted advertising, products and services. Advertisements that appear on this web site are delivered to users by our advertising partners. Our advertising partners may set cookies.

Security

Meetforeal member accounts are secured by member-created passwords. Meetforeal takes precautions to insure that member account information is kept private. Please note that we cannot guarantee the security of member account information. Unauthorized entry or use, hardware or software failure, and other factors may compromise the security of member information at any time.

Payment Security

All credit card and laser card details submitted Meetforeal are secure as Meetforeal uses Paypal to process your payments with the credit card service included, therefore none of your credit card details are stored in our servers.

Use of Cookies

Cookies are a standard Internet technology, and many major web sites use them to provide useful features for their members. Cookies allow us to store and retrieve login information on a user's system. They provide us with data that we can use to improve the quality of our service and make it easier to use. Meetforeal might not work satisfactory without cookies being enabled.

Choice/Opt-Out

Meetforeal regularly sends email to its members in order to inform them of new services or upcoming events, improvements, etc. Our members can opt-out of receiving communications from us by suspending their membership. To do this you can send an e-mail to admin@meetforeal.com or you can log onto your profile page and click on the 'Delete Account' option.

Changes in our privacy policy

From time to time we may update this Privacy Policy. You should visit this page periodically to review any such changes to the privacy policy. Your continued use of this website or our service and/or continued provision of personally identifiable information to us will be subject to the terms of the then-current Privacy Policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this website or your dealings with this website, you can contact us at admin@meetforeal.com
