What are meetforeal (with one 'r') events?

There's a lot we don't know about the world. Too much in fact.

But all knowledge is connected.

So how better to be introduced to unfamiliar topics, and discover more about familiar ones, than by meeting new people from cross-disciplined backgrounds

But don't you like to do more than just LISTEN to people talking about the wonderful things in the world?

It's cool to be able to DISCUSS and SHARE ideas with enthusiastic people who want the same thing.

This not only leads to inspiration.
It enables all of us to meet people we may otherwise never meet.

So we at meetforeal design, organise, promote and hold events for people like YOU, so we can all
be exposed to diverse, mind-tingling topics


have thought provoking conversations & share ideas across disciplines



make connections with people you may otherwise never meet

Any evidence?

What are the benefits of joining meetforeal.com?

We love technology. We are in the midst of building cool applications to make all of this better. Our first iteration is called CrowdScanner, and you can find out all about it, here

In the meantime, sign up is free and as a member you will
  • Never miss an event
  • Be able to connect with people in your area
  • Check out who's coming to events
  • Get discounts on tickets (at the moment all meetforeal events are free)
  • Watch videos of events you couldn't attend (currently we are running brucnhes, so no videos of these, but we have some great talks from 2009)
