Meetforeal

In 2007, we landed into a settled life with seemingly little room for new thoughts or ideas, after a time in our lives when there was constant movement and growth and change. Frustrated that we were no longer learning, or being triggered by intense conversations with others; and acknowledging the difficulties involved with getting to know your new community after moving house, we decided to design our own solution.

What is the aim of meetforeal?

We believe that real life cannot be beaten for intensity of conversations and depth of connection with others. Our deep intent is to create safe and open spaces for open minded people to connect and communicate based on what they are most passionate about, regardless of age, relationship status or otherwise, in real life.

How?

We are designing events for people who are passionate about specific themes, be it art, music, science, health or sport. If you are passionate about something, then there will be an event to suit you. We have asked experts to join us, to enrich the experience, and give you something to talk about as you are welcomed into the space.

Then why do you have a website?

Because we love technology too! The web is a fundamental part of our social lives and so we have developed a high-tech Java web-based application to be used as a tool, to empower our users, and enhance their overall experience of meetforeal.

What Meetforeal is not!

A Dating Site.
